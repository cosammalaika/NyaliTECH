Privacy Policy for Hymns Alive

Effective Date: April 2026 Developed by NyaliTECH

Hymns Alive is developed and maintained by NyaliTECH. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Hymns Alive does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.

  • No account registration is required
  • No personal information is requested
  • No location data is collected
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used

2. How the App Works

Hymns Alive is a fully offline application. All hymn content is stored locally on your device and does not require an internet connection to function.

3. Data Sharing

Since we do not collect any user data, we do not share any information with third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Hymns Alive does not integrate with third-party services that collect user data.

5. Children’s Privacy

Hymns Alive is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or any users.

6. Security

Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data exposure from the app itself.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

8. Contact Us
