Who We Are

Founded in 2024 under the name C&J Creative Solutions, our company began as a small team with a big dream: to leverage technology to solve real-world problems and drive positive change. In April 2025, we rebranded as NyaliTECH to reflect our evolution into a bold, future-focused tech company with a renewed identity and expanded vision.

NyaliTECH is a Zambian-owned Private Limited Company, proudly established by Zambians with a heart for innovation and a mission to create employment opportunities, especially for the youth. We believe in building local capacity, nurturing talent, and contributing to the digital transformation of Zambia and beyond.

Our team consists of passionate developers, designers, strategists, and tech enthusiasts committed to excellence, creativity, and user-centric solutions. From software development and web platforms to mobile apps and digital branding we aim to deliver technology that is not only smart and scalable but also meaningful and inclusive.

At NyaliTECH, we believe that technology should work for people. That’s why we’re driven by impact, inspired by community, and powered by purpose. We’re not just building products we’re building possibilities.
